Flip 'Em Off!

Flip 'Em Off! is a puzzle game with the deceptively simple goal of turning every light on or off.

Flipping a light switch also has the side effect of toggling the lights of your adjacent neighbors, making completing a game both strategic and rewarding.

Try to complete each puzzle in as few tries as possible!

Features

  • 3 grid sizes
  • simple, intuitive user interface
  • smooth animation and transitions
  • no fuss, jump directly into a game
  • pick up and play where you left off at any time
  • satisfying sound effects and end game
