Clax HD

Clax HD is an addictive puzzle game where you must use all of your cunning to remove every ball from the board.

Clax HD brings all of the color matching greatness of the original, but provides a completely new experience. Rewritten from the ground up, specifically for the iPad with several new features and improvements. Including Gorgeous new 3d and 2d graphics.

Rotating the iPad causes balls to restack to the new orientation, adding an entirely new level of stragegy!

Try your hand at Clax HD today!

Features

  • 8 difficulties
  • 3 ball sizes
  • 2 ball styles
  • Full rotation support (there is no 'up')
  • Local score system
  • Post scores to Facebook! (twitter integration coming)
  • Hover over balls to see what will be removed
  • Shake the iPad to quickly start a new game
  • Games in progress are saved and reloaded on start


Coming soon to the iPad App Store!
